# Anonymized Random Subsample of a DEKA Study

Randomly subsampled data from a DEKA study, containing three variables
(q120, q117, q118) related to assets and income. The data only serves as
illustrative example for the handling of user-defined missing values.

## Usage

``` r
deka_sample
```

## Format

### `deka_sample`

A data frame with 1,000 rows and 3 columns:

- q120:

  Household assets; 15 categories and 977: "keine Angabe"

- q117:

  Household net income; 13 cateories and 777: "keine Angabe"

- q118:

  Individual net income; 14 categories and 777: "keine Angabe"
